Privacy Policy for AskFiles

Last updated: April 23, 2026

AskFiles is a file manager application for Android.

Information We Collect

AskFiles does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

File Access

AskFiles requests access to your device storage solely to provide file management functionality. Your files are never uploaded, shared, or transmitted anywhere without your explicit action.

Authentication

If you choose to connect Google Drive or Microsoft OneDrive, authentication is handled directly by Google and Microsoft respectively. We receive a temporary access token stored only on your device. We do not store your name, email address, or any account information on our servers.

Purchases

In-app purchases are handled by RevenueCat and Google Play. We do not store your payment information. RevenueCat's privacy policy can be found at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.

Third Party Services

  • Google Play Services
  • RevenueCat (purchase management)
  • Microsoft Graph API (OneDrive connectivity, optional)
  • Google Drive API (optional)

Children's Privacy

AskFiles is not directed at children under 13.

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted at this URL.
